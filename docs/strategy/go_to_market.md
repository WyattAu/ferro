# Ferro Go-to-Market Strategy

## Overview

This document outlines the go-to-market strategy for Ferro.

## Target Segments

### Primary Segments
1. **Privacy-Conscious Enterprises**
   - Size: 100-10,000 employees
   - Industries: Healthcare, Finance, Legal
   - Needs: Data sovereignty, compliance, security

2. **Education Institutions**
   - Size: 1,000-50,000 students
   - Types: Universities, School Districts
   - Needs: Budget, control, integration

3. **Government Organizations**
   - Size: 100-10,000 employees
   - Types: Federal, State, Local
   - Needs: Security, compliance, sovereignty

### Secondary Segments
1. **Small-Medium Businesses**
   - Size: 10-100 employees
   - Industries: Technology, Professional Services
   - Needs: Cost, simplicity, integration

2. **Developers**
   - Size: Individual or small teams
   - Types: Indie developers, Startups
   - Needs: Open source, API, customization

## Pricing Strategy

### Free Tier (Open Source)
- **Features:** Core CalDAV/CardDAV
- **Support:** Community
- **Target:** Developers, small teams
- **Price:** Free

### Pro Tier
- **Features:** + Enterprise features, premium support
- **Support:** Email, chat
- **Target:** SMBs, education
- **Price:** $10-50/user/month

### Enterprise Tier
- **Features:** + Custom features, dedicated support
- **Support:** Phone, dedicated account manager
- **Target:** Large enterprises, government
- **Price:** Custom pricing

### Enterprise Plus Tier
- **Features:** + Custom development, SLA
- **Support:** 24/7, on-site
- **Target:** Critical deployments
- **Price:** Custom pricing

## Sales Strategy

### Self-Service (Free + Pro)
- **Channel:** Website, GitHub
- **Process:** Sign up, self-service onboarding
- **Support:** Community, documentation

### Assisted Sales (Pro + Enterprise)
- **Channel:** Sales team
- **Process:** Demo, proposal, contract
- **Support:** Account manager

### Enterprise Sales (Enterprise + Plus)
- **Channel:** Sales team + partners
- **Process:** Discovery, proposal, negotiation, contract
- **Support:** Dedicated team
