# Ferro Business Model

## Overview

This document outlines the sustainable business model for Ferro, a 100% Rust storage orchestrator and self-hosted alternative to Nextcloud.

## Revenue Streams

### 1. Open Source (Free)
- **Product:** Core CalDAV/CardDAV
- **Support:** Community
- **Target:** Developers, small teams
- **Revenue:** $0
- **Purpose:** Community building, market awareness

### 2. Premium Support (Subscription)
- **Product:** + Premium support
- **Support:** Email, chat, phone
- **Target:** SMBs, education
- **Revenue:** $10-50/user/month
- **Purpose:** Revenue generation, customer retention

### 3. Enterprise License (Subscription)
- **Product:** + Enterprise features
- **Support:** Dedicated support
- **Target:** Enterprise, government
- **Revenue:** Custom pricing
- **Purpose:** High-value customers, revenue growth

### 4. Enterprise Plus (Custom)
- **Product:** + Custom development
- **Support:** 24/7, on-site
- **Target:** Critical deployments
- **Revenue:** Custom pricing
- **Purpose:** Strategic customers, revenue stability

### 5. Professional Services (Project-Based)
- **Product:** Implementation, customization
- **Support:** Project-based
- **Target:** All segments
- **Revenue:** Hourly/project rates
- **Purpose:** Revenue diversification, customer success

### 6. Training & Certification (One-Time)
- **Product:** Training courses, certifications
- **Support:** Self-paced, instructor-led
- **Target:** All segments
- **Revenue:** Course fees, certification fees
- **Purpose:** Revenue diversification, community building

## Pricing Strategy

### Free Tier
- **Price:** $0
- **Features:** Core CalDAV/CardDAV
- **Support:** Community
- **Target:** Developers, small teams

### Pro Tier
- **Price:** $10-50/user/month
- **Features:** + Premium support, enterprise features
- **Support:** Email, chat
- **Target:** SMBs, education

### Enterprise Tier
- **Price:** Custom
- **Features:** + Dedicated support, custom features
- **Support:** Phone, dedicated account manager
- **Target:** Enterprise, government

### Enterprise Plus Tier
- **Price:** Custom
- **Features:** + Custom development, SLA
- **Support:** 24/7, on-site
- **Target:** Critical deployments

## Cost Structure

### Fixed Costs
- **Development:** Salaries, tools, infrastructure
- **Operations:** Hosting, monitoring, support
- **Marketing:** Content, events, advertising
- **Administration:** Legal, accounting, HR

### Variable Costs
- **Support:** Per-customer support costs
- **Infrastructure:** Per-customer hosting costs
- **Sales:** Commissions, travel
- **Partners:** Revenue sharing

### Cost Optimization
- **Automation:** Reduce manual operations
- **Self-Service:** Reduce support costs
- **Open Source:** Reduce development costs
- **Partners:** Reduce sales costs

### Market Risks
- **Competition:** Established players
- **Adoption:** Slow market uptake
- **Pricing:** Price pressure

### Operational Risks
- **Talent:** Hiring challenges
- **Infrastructure:** Scaling issues
- **Support:** Quality maintenance

### Financial Risks
- **Cash Flow:** Negative cash flow
