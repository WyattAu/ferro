# Ferro Market Entry Strategy

## Overview

This document outlines the market entry strategy for Ferro.

## Market Entry Approach

### Phase 1: Beachhead (0-6 months)
- **Target:** Developers, small teams
- **Geography:** North America, Europe
- **Channels:** GitHub, developer communities
- **Product:** Free tier (open source)

### Phase 2: Expansion (6-12 months)
- **Target:** SMBs, education
- **Geography:** North America, Europe, Asia Pacific
- **Channels:** Content marketing, partnerships
- **Product:** Pro tier

### Phase 3: Enterprise (12-24 months)
- **Target:** Enterprise, government
- **Geography:** Global
- **Channels:** Sales team, partners
- **Product:** Enterprise tier

### Phase 4: Leadership (24+ months)
- **Target:** Market dominance
- **Geography:** Global
- **Channels:** Analyst relations, industry events
- **Product:** Full product line

## Channel Strategy

### Direct Sales
- **Target:** Enterprise, large organizations
- **Process:** Discovery, demo, proposal, contract
- **Team:** Account executives, sales engineers

### Partner Sales
- **Target:** SMBs, education
- **Process:** Partner enablement, joint sales
- **Partners:** Resellers, consultants, SIs

### Self-Service
- **Target:** Developers, small teams
- **Process:** Website, GitHub, documentation
- **Support:** Community, documentation
