# Ferro Vertical Markets Strategy

## Overview

This document outlines the strategy for vertical market expansion.

## Healthcare Market

### Market Size
- **US Healthcare IT:** $50B+
- **CalDAV/CardDAV in Healthcare:** $500M+
- **Growth Rate:** 15-20% CAGR

### Customer Needs
- **HIPAA Compliance:** Data protection, audit logging
- **Data Sovereignty:** Control over patient data
- **Interoperability:** Integration with EHR systems
- **Security:** Encryption, access controls

### Product Adaptation
- **HIPAA Compliance:** Audit logging, encryption, access controls
- **EHR Integration:** HL7/FHIR support
- **Mobile Access:** Secure mobile clients
- **Reporting:** Compliance reporting

### Go-to-Market
- **Channels:** Healthcare IT partners, conferences
- **Content:** HIPAA compliance guides, case studies
- **Pricing:** Enterprise pricing, volume discounts
- **Support:** Dedicated healthcare support team

### Timeline
- **Month 1-3:** Research, planning
- **Month 4-6:** Product adaptation
- **Month 7-9:** Partner enablement
- **Month 10-12:** Market entry
